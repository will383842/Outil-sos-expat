05 — Journalist FAQ

The most frequently
asked questions

How is the callback in under 5 minutes guaranteed?
The platform only displays providers who are available in real time. As soon as the client chooses, the provider receives an immediate notification and is contractually required to call back in under 5 minutes. Profiles that do not meet this deadline are suspended.
Is SOS-Expat.com only for emergencies?
No. SOS-Expat.com covers all uses — emergencies of course, but also advice, opinions, support in all imaginable sectors: legal, medical, tax, housing, translation, education, banking, repairs and many others.
How does the business model work?
SOS-Expat.com applies connection fees, displayed transparently before any connection. No subscription, no hidden fees, no commission taken on the provider's fees.
Is the service reserved for French expats?
No — the service is universal. SOS-Expat.com is accessible to all nationalities, in all languages. A Japanese person in Paris, a Brazilian in Dubai or an American in Bangkok can use the platform in their own language.
What types of providers are found on the platform?
Experts in all fields: legal, medical, tax, housing, translation, education, banking, repairs — and any other sector. Providers are visible online with their specialties, languages spoken, countries of intervention and client reviews.
Is the platform available in multiple languages?
Yes — 9 languages natively: French, English, Spanish, German, Portuguese, Arabic, Simplified Chinese, Hindi and Russian. The provider network covers many other languages depending on the country.
How do you become a provider on SOS-Expat.com?
Experts and helpers register at sos-expat.com. After profile validation, they define their availability, countries of intervention, languages, specialties and rates. Connection fees are only charged when an actual connection takes place.
What is the difference from travel insurance?
Travel insurance reimburses after. SOS-Expat.com acts during — in under 5 minutes, with the provider the client has chosen themselves. The two services are complementary.
