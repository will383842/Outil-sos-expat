06

Questions essentielles

Est-ce vraiment gratuit ?
Yes, entirely. Registration, profile creation and going online cost nothing. SOS-Expat only charges a connection fee when a call actually takes place. Not before. Not otherwise.
What happens after the first call?
Any future case — consultation, representation, follow-up — is managed directly between you and your client, outside the platform. SOS-Expat does not intervene. This client belongs entirely to you, with your full fees.
Do I need to be available all the time?
No. You activate and deactivate your availability with one click. No volume commitment, no minimum hours, no constraints. You are master of your schedule.
How and when am I paid?
The client pays directly through the platform at the end of the call. The amount is transferred to you instantly, minus the connection fee. No waiting, no invoice to issue.
