Key questions — for your article

What exactly is it?
An application that connects, in under 5 minutes, any expatriate, traveler or vacationer in difficulty with the lawyer or expat helper who speaks their language — wherever they are in the world. All nationalities, all languages, 197 countries, 24/7.
What's new about it?
Before SOS-Expat.com: nothing. No global emergency service, in all languages, available 24/7, with expert choice and guaranteed callback in 5 minutes. The client chooses themselves — this is a world first.
Pourquoi "universel" ?
SOS-Expat.com does not target one nationality or one language. The platform was designed universal from day one: 9 languages natively, 197 countries, all nationalities. French, English, Arabic, Chinese, Hindi, Portuguese, German, Japanese, Korean — and any language in the world.
The business model?
Commission charged only when an actual call takes place. Experts freely set their rates, paid directly by clients. Subsequent cases belong entirely to them. Scalable model: each registered provider expands the network at no marginal cost.
